It is not my dream to be popuplar, I just wanna make music. With simple instruments,free DAW, and very minimal time to record, I have published some songs in many online music platforms. Im not boasting, just sharing some facts. Whenever I make songs, I always put my self in someone else's shoes. Or, I would create a situation in life by starting with the question "What if?". Most of the time, the rythm or beat comes first, then the lyrics.

What inspires your music?
My mood, feelings, imaginations, and daily events in our lives.

What is your genre of music?
I really don't have a specific genre for my music, as long as the beat, rythm, and tune is cool, then im good with it.

Who are your influences?
Red Hot Chilli Peppers, I used to memorize their songs back when I was in high school.

What do you want your listeners to learn from you?
I really don't expect anyone to learn from me, but I could say, life is short when you worry, and you forget about time when you have fun.

How would you describe your music to those who never heard it before?
My music is raw, and cool, its not always the same beat you hear from this days. It's a mixture of alternative, rap, reggae, and rock music.

Do you write and produce your own music?
Yeah, everything in my songs is done by me.

What does it really feel like to be and indedendent artist?
Well it feels alone, you know, i have nobody to knod my head with while mixing the songs. And doing everything from scratch requires alot of time. But it pays off in the end.

What is your favorite part of the song writing process?
The beggining of creating the beat and tune, how they sound like in my head. And of course, recording those sounds is one of the best part.

What can we expect from you in future?
You gonna hear more of my songs coming, and I'm never gonna stop, I may not be the one to sing them, but i'll make sure i'll be producing those songs. it may not be a hit, but atleast it is out there and not just a rotten imagination.
